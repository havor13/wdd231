Ghanaian E‑Learning Resource Hub — Site Plan

Author: Sampson Havor • Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Site Name

Ghanaian E‑Learning Resource Hub — chosen to reflect accessible, curated learning resources and strategies for learners in Ghana and beyond.

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for Ghanaian learners by offering:

  • Curated links to trusted online learning platforms
  • Study strategies tailored for Ghanaian students
  • Accessibility guidance to make education inclusive

Scenarios

  • Where can Ghanaian learners find trusted online learning platforms?
  • What study and accessibility tips will help me succeed in my courses?

Color Scheme

  • Deep Green (#006400): Headings, navigation, buttons, borders
  • Gold (#FFD700): Highlights, hover states, footer accents

These colors are applied in this document to meet rubric requirements.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings and navigation
  • Roboto: Used for body text and lists
